What is XR (Extended Reality) Extended Reality (XR) is an umbrella term that includes all immersive technologies that blend or replace the physical world with digital content. It covers Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR), each offering different levels of immersion and interaction. In the context of this tool, XR defines the overall direction of the project, influencing every decision that follows, from how content is created to how it is experienced and delivered to users.
